About Our Company

Trusted Dental Supplier Since 1995

SHANTI DENTAL RAVI & CO has been serving dental professionals across Hyderabad and Telangana for over two decades. We specialize in providing high-quality dental instruments, equipment, and consumables to both wholesale and retail customers.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer service has made us a preferred partner for dental clinics, hospitals, and practitioners throughout the region.

With our extensive inventory and deep industry knowledge, we ensure that dental professionals have access to the tools they need to provide exceptional patient care.
